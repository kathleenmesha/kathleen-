About Me

I'm Kathleen Binayao Butad, 19-years old living in Bugcaon Lantapan Bukidnon. I’m excited to share a little about myself, including my hobbies, favorites, and goals for the next eight years.

My mission is to live with purpose, kindness, and honesty. I want to grow as a person, face challenges with a positive attitude, and build strong relationships. I believe in enjoying life, staying true to my values, and helping others whenever I can.

What Drives Me

What drives me is the desire to grow, learn, and experience life to its fullest. I’m passionate about exploring new opportunities, connecting with others, and continuously improving myself. I find motivation in embracing new experiences and enjoying the journey of personal growth.

My Journey

My journey has been about discovering who I am and what I want to achieve in life. From a young age, I’ve always valued learning and self-improvement. Now, as a student at Bukidnon State University, I am building on that foundation, excited to continue growing, taking on new challenges, and working towards my future goals.
