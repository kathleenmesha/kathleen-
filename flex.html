My Proud Moments

I was proud to graduate as Salutatorian, delivering the Welcome Address, and sharing the moment with my parents.

I had the honor of leading the Pledge of Loyalty and being in the top 3 made the ceremony unforgettable, especially with my parents by my side.

Graduating with honors in SHS was a rewarding achievement, made even more special by celebrating it with my parents as always.

My family is my biggest support, always guiding and inspiring me with their love and encouragement.

My friends have always been supportive, sharing laughs and unforgettable moments.

My cousins are not just family, but also great friends who always support and care for me.

My 18th birthday was a special celebration with family and friends, marking the start of adulthood.

The prom night was an unforgettable celebration of our high school journey.

After 17 long years, I finally had the chance to visit my birthplace, Leyte. It was a heartfelt trip, reconnecting with the place where i was born.
